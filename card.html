Cat

Cat eyes have a special reflective layer called tapetum, reflecting light back into the retina.
This is one of the things that give them superior night vision

  • Scientific Name:Felis catus
  • Average Weight: 4 kg
  • Average Lifespan: 12 years
  • Habitat: Almost everywhere where humans are found

Cats first started living with humans some 80000 years ago. In ancient egypt they were worshiped as gods. This is something they have never forgotten. Cats have even inspired great poetry such as the book about cats by T.S Elliot.
